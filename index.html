Our Story

A Legacy of Excellence

Nestled in the rolling hills of Tuscany, Villa Rosata has been producing world-class wines for over three decades. Our founder, Marco Rosata, believed that great wine begins in the vineyard, a philosophy we honor to this day.

Every bottle tells the story of our land, our climate, and the passion of our winemakers. From hand-harvested grapes to careful aging in our centuries-old cellars, we craft wines that capture the essence of Italian winemaking tradition.

Today, the third generation of the Rosata family continues this legacy, blending time-honored techniques with modern innovation to produce wines of extraordinary character and depth.

Where Great Wine Begins

Our 35-hectare estate sits at an elevation of 350 meters, where the Mediterranean climate and mineral-rich soil create perfect conditions for our vines. The warm days and cool nights allow our grapes to develop intense flavors while maintaining their natural acidity.

We practice sustainable viticulture, working in harmony with nature to produce grapes of the highest quality. Each vine is tended by hand, and we harvest at the precise moment when the grapes reach optimal ripeness.
